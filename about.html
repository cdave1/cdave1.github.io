I live and work in Wellington, New Zealand. I also frequently spend time in the San Francisco Bay Area on business.

I am currently working on new projects.

In 2013 I was co-founder of a company called SocialBox. We got funded by Y Combinator.

Between 2010 and 2013, I was founder and CTO of PlayCoMo, a company based in the USA that makes mobile phone games.

Before PlayCoMo I made indie mobile phone games under the moniker @hackdirt. I also did ad-hoc mobile phone apps for companies like Subway, Pizza-Hut, Wagamama, and Royal Bank of Scotland.

Before this I was lead developer at education startup Learning Source, and before that I wrote boring financial modelling software for big faceless corporations.
