I live and work in Wellington, New Zealand. I am currently working on new projects with @kapsy1312.

In 2013 I was co-founder of a company called SocialBox. We got funded by Y Combinator.

Between 2010 and 2013, I was founder and CTO of PlayCoMo, a company that makes mobile phone games.

Before PlayCoMo I made indie mobile phone games under the moniker @hackdirt. I also built iOS apps for companies like Subway NZ, Pizza Hut, Wagamama, and Royal Bank of Scotland.

From 2007 to 2008 I was lead developer at awesome education software startup Learning Source. LS powers a training platform for a whole bunch of schools and companies in New Zealand and Australia, including Xero.

Before all of this I wrote financial modelling software and business applications for big faceless corporations. I also dropped out of computer science grad school.
