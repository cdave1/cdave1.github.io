I live and work in Wellington, New Zealand. Check it out:



Since early 2014, I have been working on startup called Vast with @kapsy1312. Vast is a company that is making new tools for interacting with computers in virtual environments.

In 2013 I was co-founder of a company called SocialBox. We got funded by Y Combinator.

Between 2010 and 2013, I was founder and CTO of PlayCoMo, a company that makes mobile phone games. I ran the company's office out of Wellington, New Zealand.

Before PlayCoMo I made indie mobile phone games under the moniker @hackdirt. I also built iOS apps for various companies in New Zealand, Australia, and the UK (including Subway NZ, Pizza Hut, Wagamama, and Royal Bank of Scotland).

From 2007 to 2008 I was lead developer at awesome education software startup Learning Source. LS powers a training platform for a whole bunch of schools and companies in New Zealand and Australia, including Xero.

Before all of this I wrote financial modelling software and business applications for various companies in New Zealand.
